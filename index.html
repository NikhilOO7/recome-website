Privacy First

Your data stays on your device

RecoMe has no servers, no analytics, no tracking. Everything runs locally in your browser.

What we store locally

  • ✓ Your blocked & liked topics
  • ✓ Interaction history (your feedback)
  • ✓ Knowledge graph (topic relationships)
  • ✓ Extension settings
  • ✓ Achievement progress

What we never collect

  • ✗ Personal information
  • ✗ Browsing history
  • ✗ Analytics or telemetry
  • ✗ Tracking pixels
  • ✗ Data sold to third parties
