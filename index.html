Under the Hood

Powered by a
learning brain

RecoMe doesn't just follow rules. It builds a memory, detects patterns, maps knowledge, and gets smarter with every interaction.

🧠

Memory System

Records your feedback, searches, and implicit signals. Patterns fade naturally with a 30-day half-life, so RecoMe adapts as your interests evolve.

📈

Pattern Detection

After 3 similar events, RecoMe creates mid-term cards that influence future scoring. "Avoids clickbait" or "prefers Sony" emerge automatically from your behavior.

🌐

Knowledge Graph

Maps relationships between topics, brands, and categories. Blocking "spam" reduces "phishing" too. Pre-seeded and grows with use.

👁

Implicit Learning

Learns from how you browse — dwell time, scroll patterns, revisits, click-throughs. No buttons needed; your behavior is the signal.

💡

Proactive Insights

Detects research fatigue, preference drift, and cross-platform patterns. Surfaces suggestions before you ask — "You've looked at 12 laptops. Want me to narrow it down?"

🔗

Cross-Platform Sync

Your preferences carry everywhere. Block a topic once and it's hidden on YouTube, Amazon, Twitter, and every other site. The linker strengthens connections daily.

Privacy First

Your data stays on your device

RecoMe has no servers, no analytics, no tracking. Everything runs locally in your browser.

  • ✓ Your blocked & liked topics

What we never collect
